How to make an exchange on your service?

All operations in the AlfaBit exchange office are carried out automatically under the control of operators.

  1. Select the exchange direction in the “Give” column and the “Receive” column
  2. Fill in all the details and click on the button "Go to the exchange"
  3. Check the correctness of your details and click on the "Proceed to payment" button. In your payment system, check the correctness of the AlfaBit service wallet and the comment on the transfer. You need to make a payment within 17 minutes. Also, a message about the status of the application will be sent to the contact email. If for some reason you did not manage to make a payment within the specified period of time, then write to the operator to solve the problem.
  4. After a successful exchange operation, please give your opinion about the AlfaBit service.
How long does the exchange take?

Electronic currency exchanges are instant. Cryptocurrency transfer time depends on the speed of network confirmations, usually takes 5-30 minutes after sending. If the blockchain network is busy, this time may increase. In cases where the transaction has already been uploaded to the network, our service cannot affect the speed of sending funds.

How long to wait for Cashin replenishment

Usually applications in the direction of Cashin take from 30 minutes to 4 hours. In rare cases, up to 8 hours. Applications that were created after 20:00 are processed the next day.

Why was my order rejected?

An order may be rejected in the following cases:

  1. You have not made a payment within 16 minutes 40 seconds from the moment of registration of the order;
  2. You transferred the wrong amount and the system does not recognize your payment;
  3. You transferred an amount of Bitcoin less than the minimum recommended commission according to the order. Orders with Bitcoin, paid for an amount less than the minimum recommended commission of the system, are not subject to execution and return;
  4. You have specified incorrect details, according to which the system cannot make a payment;
  5. You entered an invalid email address.
I received an email from your service about the replacement of the order details. What does it mean and what should I do?

According to the user agreement of our service p.9.4.1: "To change the beneficiary's details, the User submits an application to change the details in an email sent to support_exchange@alfabit.org. The letter must be sent from the email address specified when creating the order in which the User wishes to change the details. The letter should specify the number of the order, the old requisites from the order and the new requisites for receiving funds".

How does the verification of bank cards go in your service?

To verify a virtual card, you need to open virtual card in your banking application on your phone, so that you can see the card number in full (other data can be hidden), take a picture of your phone in front of the site with your order open and attach this photo to your order through a special form on the website. To verify a physical card you should take a photo of your card; your card number should be seen against the background of the screen with your open order on the site of our service. Then you need to attach this photo via a special form in the web application window.  At this time verification is required only for cards of Tinkoff Bank and Sberbank. If you have any questions you can ask them at any time in the chat of technical support.

Can I cancel the exchange if I have already paid the deposit amount?

If you have already paid, you cannot cancel the exchange request. You can create a new request for the reverse exchange operation, but in this case all commissions are paid by the client.

Why is my order not executed?

The order may have the status "Pending" in these cases:

  • Your coins are waiting to receive the required number of network confirmations. The speed of confirmation depends on the commission issued by you or the resource from where you made the transfer. Once the required number of confirmations for your transaction has been received, the status of your request will change and the request will be paid in full;
  • You did not pay within the time frame from the moment the order was submitted;
  • You transferred the wrong amount and the system didn't recognize your payment;
  • You transferred an amount in the Bitcoin network, indicating an amount less than the minimum recommended fee;
  • Orders in Bitcoin, paid for an amount less than the minimum recommended commission of the system, will not be refunded;
  • You have indicated incorrect transfer details, for which the system can not make a payment. In this case it will be necessary to change the details.

For prompt resolution of any problems, please contact the technical support chat at https://alfabit.org

Is registration on your website required?

No, registration in our service is not obligatory, but after passing the registration you will be able to monitor the history of completed transactions and participate in affiliate program.

Why did I receive an amount different from that specified in my application?

Funds from you received after the expiration of the timer for payment. The rate in the order remains fixed if the payment was made by you during the time given for the payment of the order. If the 1st confirmation came after the timer for payment has expired, the service has the right to recalculate such a request.

The order is overdue, I did not have time to pay (Sberbank, Tinkoff), where can I find the details for payment?

For the safety of our customers, we do not duplicate details in the chat, they can be found in the mail from which you created the order. The letter can get into your Spam or Junk Mail folder.

What is the schedule of operation of your service?

We work 24/7. All operations are carried out automatically or semi-automatically, you can exchange money at any time convenient for you.

What services does your company provide?

AlfaBit Exchange offers automatic exchange of electronic currencies, cryptocurrency funds and fiat money.

The funds were not deposited to my wallet, although the order was successfully completed

In this case it is better to immediately contact the technical support chat. Sometimes we send funds by means of a smart contract. Some wallets may not credit funds for such transactions immediately. To resolve the problem you need to give hash of the transaction in support of your wallet and ask to credit the money to your wallet.

What is a smart contract?

A smart contract is a computer program that can automatically execute a contract if certain conditions are met. It is called a smart contract because it can execute itself rather than relying on a third party to enforce the terms of the contract. Smart contracts are often used in blockchain technology, which is a decentralized system for storing and managing data. In blockchain, smart contracts are stored in a public ledger, which means they can be accessed and verified by anyone.
